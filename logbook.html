Current Digital Logbooks Miss the Point

They weren't designed by pilots who've lived through the pain.

Chronological Prison

Try adding a flight from 10 years ago and watch the chaos. Most digital logbooks force entries in order, making catch-up logging a nightmare. Paper logbook converts with thousands of entries? Good luck.

Military Conversion Nightmare

Transitioning from military to civilian? Your NATOPS or SHARP format doesn't translate. Hours are categorized differently. Nobody's built the conversion engine you need.

Paper-to-Digital Cliff

You've got 40 years of paper entries. Current apps make you choose: transcribe everything or start fresh and lose your history. Neither option respects your time or your records.

Subscription Hostage

Your data lives on someone else's server. Stop paying, lose access. No export, no local backup, no control. That's not ownership—that's renting your own flight history.

AeroEdge Logbook

A digital logbook that actually solves the problems pilots face, not the problems developers imagine.

Smart OCR Digitization

Snap photos of your paper logbook pages. Our OCR reads the entries, but you're the final authority. Every interpreted entry queues for your review—approve, correct, or reject. No algorithm overwrites your records without your say-so. 40 years of paper? Digitize it in batches, on your schedule, with full control.

Fully Configurable for Any Aircrew

Not locked into a fixed-wing GA template. Configure your logbook for any role— helicopter pilots, flight engineers, loadmasters, sensor operators, military crew positions. Add custom columns, categories, and time types. Your career, your logbook structure.

  • Out-of-order entry: Add a flight from any date. It auto-sorts correctly and updates all totals. Transcribe 40 years of paper entries at whatever pace you want.
  • Paper View mode: Horizontal layout that mirrors your physical logbook. See entries the way you're used to seeing them.
  • Military conversion engine: NATOPS/SHARP format to FAA standard. The translation that transitioning military pilots actually need.
  • Smart validation: Catches common errors—sim time logged as flight time, dual given vs received confusion, impossible combinations.
  • Currency tracking: 6-hits, BFR, medical, IPC with proactive alerts. Know when you're about to go non-current.
  • 8710 auto-population: Direct export for checkride applications. The math is done for you.

The Greybeard Principle

Every design decision must pass this test.

Imagine a pilot who's used paper logbooks for 40 years. They're skeptical of digital anything. They've seen apps come and go, companies shut down, data disappear. They value their records and their autonomy.

That pilot must be willing to use this tool.

Not because they're forced to. Not because "digital is the future." But because the tool genuinely respects their needs and improves their workflow.

What This Means in Practice

  • No forced account creation to try the tool
  • Local-first data storage options—your data on your device
  • One-time purchase available, not subscription-only
  • Full data export in standard formats, always
  • Paper View interfaces that mirror familiar layouts
  • Respect for expertise—not dumbed-down interfaces
