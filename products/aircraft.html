AeroEdge Aircraft

Maintenance tracking that updates itself when you log flights.

The Aircraft Tracking Problem

Spreadsheets and whiteboards don't cut it anymore.

"When is the annual due? Did anyone update the hobbs?"

Aircraft times live in one place, maintenance records in another, and nobody remembers to update either after a flight.

"There's a squawk on the whiteboard. Is it still open?"

Discrepancy tracking on whiteboards gets erased. Paper squawk sheets get lost. Nobody knows what's deferred and when it expires.

"We're due for 100-hour but nobody noticed."

Hour-based inspections creep up. Without automated tracking, you find out when you're already overdue. That's an airworthiness problem.

"Is there an AD that affects this aircraft?"

AD tracking is a mess of spreadsheets and paper files. Recurring ADs need countdown tracking. One-time ADs need compliance verification.

How It Works

Automatic Time Sync

Log a flight in the logbook, aircraft times update automatically. No double entry. No "did you update the hobbs?" questions.

What Syncs:
  • Hobbs time from flight entries
  • Tach time (if tracked)
  • Fuel used and added
  • Flight cycles / landings
  • Real-time countdown to inspections
  • Automatic alerts at thresholds
  • History of all time changes
  • Multiple aircraft support

Complete Maintenance Visibility

Inspection Tracking

Never miss an inspection again.

  • Annual - 12 calendar month countdown
  • 100-hour - Hobbs-based with 10-hour overfly
  • Progressive - Phase tracking support
  • Transponder - 24-month cycle
  • Pitot-static/altimeter - IFR compliance
  • ELT - Annual + battery tracking

AD Compliance

Airworthiness directive tracking done right.

  • AD database - Search by make/model
  • Recurring ADs - Hour/calendar countdowns
  • One-time ADs - Compliance documentation
  • Method of compliance - Track what was done
  • Alert thresholds - Advance warning

Squawk Management

Track discrepancies from report to resolution.

  • Severity levels - Info, minor, major, grounding
  • Photo attachments - Document the issue
  • Resolution tracking - What was done, by whom
  • MEL deferrals - Expiration tracking
  • Airworthiness impact - Auto-ground for critical

Ecosystem Integration

Connected to everything.

  • Logbook - Times sync from flight entries
  • Scheduler - Blocks aircraft when grounded
  • Dispatch - Pre-flight status at a glance
  • Alerts - Push notifications for due items
  • Multiple users - Club/school visibility

Who It's For

Owner-Operators

Track your aircraft's maintenance without spreadsheets. Know when things are due. Keep records organized for your A&P.

Flying Clubs

Multiple pilots, shared aircraft, transparent maintenance status. Everyone sees the same information.

Flight Schools

Fleet management that integrates with scheduling. Ground aircraft automatically when squawks require it.
