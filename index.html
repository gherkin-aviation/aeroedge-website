The Problem

GA pilots deserve better tools than what's available today.

Flying Blind to Performance

Most pilots never see their aircraft's true performance envelope. POH charts give you numbers, but not understanding. Where exactly does your aircraft run out of energy in a steep turn? At what altitude does climb performance disappear?

Training Tools Stuck in the Past

Flight instructors explain maneuvers with hand gestures and chalkboards. Students practice without visualizing the energy they're managing. The math exists, but the tools to make it accessible don't.

Digital Logbooks That Miss the Point

Current digital logbooks force you to enter flights in order. They don't understand military formats. They can't handle the 40-year pilot finally going digital with thousands of out-of-order entries.

No Integration, Just Silos

Your logbook doesn't talk to your aircraft tracking. Your training tools don't use your actual aircraft data. Everything is disconnected, creating more work instead of less.

EM Diagram Generator

See your aircraft's complete performance envelope. Energy-Maneuverability diagrams show exactly where you can and cannot go—the same framework used to design and fly tactical aircraft, now available for your Cessna, Cirrus, or Bonanza.

AeroEdge Exclusive: Dynamic Vmc & Dynamic Vyse

The FAA's published Vmc is a single number tested under ideal conditions. Reality is different. AeroEdge calculates Dynamic Vmc and Dynamic Vyse—showing how these critical speeds actually change with weight, density altitude, bank angle, and configuration. See your true multi-engine safety margins, not textbook abstractions.

  • 100+ aircraft presets with full aerodynamic parameters
  • Custom aircraft editor for any aircraft
  • Stall boundaries, G-limits, and Specific Excess Power regions
  • Dynamic Vmc/Vyse—real-time multi-engine critical speeds
  • Export to PNG/PDF for briefings
  • Mobile-responsive—works on any device

AeroEdge Logbook

A digital logbook designed by pilots who understand the actual pain points. Not another app that forces you into someone else's workflow.

Smart OCR Digitization

Photograph your paper logbook pages and let OCR do the heavy lifting. Every interpreted entry goes through your review before it's final—no blind trust in algorithms. You approve, correct, or reject each entry. Your logbook, your authority.

Fully Configurable

Not just for GA pilots. Configure columns, categories, and fields for any aircrew role—helicopter pilots, flight engineers, loadmasters, military crew positions. Your logbook structure, your rules.

  • Out-of-order entry: Add a flight from 10 years ago, it auto-sorts correctly
  • Paper View mode: Horizontal layout matching physical logbooks
  • Military conversion: NATOPS/SHARP format to FAA standard
  • Smart validation: Catches common logging errors automatically
  • Currency tracking: 6-hits, BFR, medical with proactive alerts
  • 8710 auto-population: Direct export for checkride applications

Built for military pilots converting thousands of hours, CFIs managing students, and paper logbook users finally going digital.
